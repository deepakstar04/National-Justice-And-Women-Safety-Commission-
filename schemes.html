Schemes

International Health Care System

The Government is dedicated to implementing an International Health Care System that ensures every citizen, irrespective of their social or economic background, has access to quality medical care. This scheme emphasizes affordable healthcare, the introduction of modern medical infrastructure, and National Justice and Women Safety Commision partnerships with global healthcare organizations to improve medical standards across the nation.

Women Safety and Protection

The Government prioritizes the safety and protection of women through initiatives that include stricter laws, rapid response systems, and community awareness campaigns. The scheme also focuses on setting up dedicated women safety cells, increasing the presence of women police personnel, and providing support for victims of abuse and violence .

Programs

Awareness Programs

Spreading knowledge and raising awareness about fundamental rights, duties, and safety measures across communities.

Women Safety Programmes

Ensuring the safety of women through awareness campaigns, self-defense training, and robust safety protocols.

Youth Awareness

Engaging the youth in community building, social responsibility, and active participation in governance policies.

Fundamental Rights and Duties Awareness

Fundamental Rights and Duties Awareness programs aim to educate individuals about their constitutional rights and responsibilities, fostering a sense of accountability and empowering communities to actively participate in a just and equitable society.

Easy Justice Policy

Easy Justice Policy aligns with awareness programs by promoting accessible legal knowledge, ensuring communities understand their rights and remedies, and enabling fair, timely resolution of disputes to uphold justice for all.

Prevention Of Rapes, Dowry System

Awareness programs contribute to the prevention of rapes and the dowry system by educating communities about legal protections, promoting gender equality, and encouraging societal change to eliminate these harmful practices.
