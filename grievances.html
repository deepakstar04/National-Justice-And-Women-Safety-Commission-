Live Cases

About This Case

Introduction

This case study presents a powerful and emotional story of a young girl, aged 16, who became a victim of digital deception and abuse. Through her experience, we aim to raise awareness about the risks women face in the digital age and the importance of taking preventive measures to ensure safety.

Case Summary

Initial Interaction

The story begins with a 16-year-old girl, Sarah (name changed), who meets a seemingly charming young man on Facebook.
Their virtual friendship grows quickly as they share personal stories and build what Sarah believes is a genuine connection.

Escalation of Trust

After months of chatting online, they decide to meet in person. Their bond deepens during subsequent meetups, eventually leading to a physical relationship.

Marriage and Reality Check

Convinced of his love, Sarah agrees to marry him despite her young age and limited life experience.
Soon after the marriage, his true nature begins to surface. He starts demanding money from Sarah, using emotional manipulation and threats of divorce.

Abuse and Infidelity

The situation worsens as he becomes verbally and emotionally abusive, pressuring Sarah for financial support she cannot provide. Sarah later discovers that he is in a relationship with another woman.

Outcome

With courage and the support of her family and friends, Sarah decides to confront her husband and leave the toxic relationship.
She reaches out to a women’s safety organization, where she receives counseling and legal support to rebuild her life.
