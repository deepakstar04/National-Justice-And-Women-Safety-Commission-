About

National Justice and Women safety Commission has been established in 2024 under act and it is been registered from Niti Aayog. Supported by the Ministry of Home Affairs and State Government, this commission is committed to serving communities and implementing initiatives across India. The Soul Purpose of this commission is to create a separate platform for the poor civilians those who don’t have the access to fight for their rights . The major issue in the society is that less education, less awareness, fear in economically weaker section people . This Commission shall play a impactful role in poor civilians life's to provide them justice for their genuine problems .

This commission is working for the nation without any cast and creed on all corners of the society.

The purpose of this commission is to transform the system and to provide help and relief on the public grievances by the help of state.

By the Support of State Government and Department of Justice the national body of this commission shall work.

Objective

In India, a new National Justice and Women Empowerment Commission has been established to bring change. Women's safety and empowerment have become focal points to combat atrocities and uplift society.


Women safety remains a critical concern in India, with the country experiencing one of the highest rates of crimes against women globally.


In India, there is a shortage of resources in key areas such as administration, healthcare, police services, and the judicial system.


This commission will operate at the grassroots level, working directly to ensure justice and safety for those in need.


Women empowerment involves granting economic, social, and educational rights without discrimination based on gender, class, religion, or social status. It is an essential prerequisite for the development and progress of a nation.
